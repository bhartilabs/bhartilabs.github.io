Privacy Policy for iReducePDF Last Updated: March 19, 2026 At Bharti Labs, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how iReducePDF ("the App") handles your information. Unlike many online tools, iReducePDF is designed as an offline-first utility, ensuring your sensitive documents stay on your device. 1. Core Privacy Principle: Local Processing The primary function of iReducePDF is to compress and manage PDF files. • Zero Uploads: All PDF processing, compression, and manipulation are performed locally on your device. • No Document Collection: We do not upload, store, or have any access to the content of the PDF files you process. Your documents never leave your phone through our App. 2. Information We Collect Automatically To support the App’s development and provide a free service, we use trusted third-party SDKs that may collect limited technical information. A. Analytics and Crash Reporting We use Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics for Firebase to improve app stability. •Data Collected: Crash logs, device model, OS version, and app performance metrics. •Purpose: To identify and fix bugs and improve the user experience. B. Advertising We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. • Data Collected: Advertising ID (GAID), device identifiers, and basic interaction data. • Purpose: To serve relevant ads and prevent fraudulent activity. • Opt-Out: You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads via your Android Device Settings (Settings > Google > Ads). 3. Permissions We Use The App requires certain permissions to function: • Storage (Read/Write): To access the PDF files you wish to compress and to save the resulting files back to your device. • Internet/Network State: To load advertisements and send anonymized crash reports. • Foreground Service/Wake Lock: To ensure PDF compression tasks are completed successfully if you switch apps or lock your screen. • Post Notifications: To alert you when a compression task is finished. 4. Data Sharing We do not sell your personal data. Technical data (like Device IDs) is shared only with our service providers (Google/Firebase) for the purposes of advertising and app performance monitoring. We may disclose information only if required by law to comply with legal obligations. 5. Data Security Even though we do not collect your documents, we value the security of the technical data processed by our third-party partners. All data transmitted by Google SDKs is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS). 6. Children’s Privacy The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. 8.
